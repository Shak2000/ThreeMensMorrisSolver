How to Play

Placement Phase: Players take turns placing their 3 pieces on empty positions.

Movement Phase: Players move their pieces to adjacent positions along the lines.

Goal: Form a line of 3 pieces (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

AI: Use the AI Move button to let the computer play for the current player.
